My Dearest Love,

If I had the gift of writing beautiful words the way poets once did, I would still fail to explain what your presence does to my heart.

Today is your birthday, and as I sit here thinking of you, I realize something I have known since the 6th class my heart has never truly belonged to anyone else. Not for a single day. Not for a single night.

· · ✦ · ·

There has not been one evening since then where I did not miss you in some quiet way.

Some people speak of love as if it arrives suddenly, like a storm. But loving you was softer than that. It was slow, certain, and endless. Like the sunrise that never forgets to return.

· · ✦ · ·

You have become a part of my every thought. Your laughter stays with me long after our conversations end. Your name lives in my mind like a favourite line from a novel I could read a thousand times and still fall in love with.

And perhaps this will sound foolishly romantic, but it is true when you are near me, I feel alive in ways I cannot explain. The world feels lighter. Breathing feels easier. Existing feels beautiful.

· · ✦ · ·

Your presence carries peace into my restless heart.

I often wonder if you know how deeply you are loved. How even after all these years, I still look at you with the same admiration, the same softness, the same quiet amazement. As if my heart recognized yours long before I understood what love truly was.

· · ✦ · ·

If life allows me one lasting happiness, I think it would simply be this: to spend ordinary days beside you. To hear your voice at the end of long evenings. To watch you smile over little things. To grow older while still loving you exactly like this.

You are, and always will be, my favourite thought.

· · ✦ · ·

So on your birthday, I do not only wish you happiness. I wish you a life filled with gentle mornings, beautiful dreams, and a love that reminds you every day how precious you are to someone.

And if destiny is kind to me, perhaps one day I will be allowed to call that happiness mine forever.

· · ✦ · ·

Happy Birthday, My beloved Momina kayani.

Yours,
Always & Completely Zulqarnain kayani.
